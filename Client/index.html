Our Story

Triveni Auto Agri Services has established itself as a trusted name in the automotive parts manufacturing industry. Located in Pune, Maharashtra, we specialize in producing high-quality suspension arms, SG iron castings, and various automotive components that meet international standards.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred supplier for leading automotive manufacturers across India. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver products that exceed expectations.
